Both parties agree to the following terms and conditions:

1. Scope of Services

Nova Digital Labs agrees to provide the following services at no development cost:

  • Professional website design.
  • Website development and setup.
  • Responsive (mobile-friendly) website.
  • Basic SEO-friendly structure.
  • Website deployment on the provided hosting platform (if applicable).
  • Technical guidance and support during setup.

2. Free Service Policy

The Client acknowledges that:

  • The website design and development services are provided free of charge by Nova Digital Labs.

3. Advertising & Revenue Model

As consideration for providing these services free of cost, the Client agrees that:

  1. Nova Digital Labs is authorized to display third-party advertisements on the Client's website.

  4. All advertising activities shall comply with the applicable laws and regulations of Nepal, including any government policies governing online advertising.

4. Future Domain & Hosting Migration

If, at any time in the future, the Client wishes to:

  • Purchase their own domain,
  • Use independent hosting,
  • Remove the existing hosted setup,

Nova Digital Labs agrees to provide reasonable technical assistance for migration, deployment, and setup. Any third-party costs (such as domain registration, hosting fees, premium services, or licensing fees) shall be borne solely by the Client.

5. Client Responsibilities

The Client agrees to:

  • Ensure that all submitted content complies with applicable laws.

Nova Digital Labs may provide technical support at its discretion. Any additional feature requests, redesigns, or major modifications may require a separate agreement.

8. Limitation of Liability

Nova Digital Labs shall not be held responsible for:

  • Downtime caused by third-party hosting providers.
  • Internet outages.
  • Third-party software failures.
  • Cyber-attacks beyond reasonable control.
  • Financial losses arising from website usage.

9. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing written notice. Upon termination:

  • Any mutually agreed obligations shall remain enforceable.
  • Advertisement services shall cease unless otherwise agreed.
  • If website migration is requested, Nova Digital Labs will provide reasonable technical assistance.

10. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and interpreted in accordance with the laws of Nepal. Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through mutual discussion. If unresolved, the matter shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the competent courts of Nepal.

This document constitutes the entire agreement between Nova Digital Labs and the Client regarding the services described herein and supersedes any prior verbal or written discussions.
